Pro Tips

Get the Full Match
Always contribute enough to get 100% of employer match. It's literally free money with instant 100% returns.
►
Max It Out
2024 limit is $23,000/year ($30,500 if 50+). Maxing out early in your career has massive compound effects.
►
Target Date Funds
If you don't know what to invest in, target date funds automatically rebalance as you age. Set it and forget it.

How It's Calculated

Your Contribution: Salary × Contribution %
Employer Match: Min(Your Contribution, Salary × Match Limit) × (Match % ÷ Match Limit)
Annual Growth: Previous Balance × (1 + Return Rate) + Total Annual Contributions
Salary Growth: Salary increases by specified % each year

Calculations assume contributions and employer match occur at year-end, and returns compound annually.
