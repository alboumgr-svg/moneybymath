What Is Working

How This Health Check Thinks

1
Cash Flow Comes First
A strong income can still hide weak finances if every dollar is already spoken for. The score checks whether your monthly system leaves room for saving, investing, and absorbing surprises.
2
Age Benchmarks Live in JSON
Median income, net worth, retirement savings, debt, and investment benchmarks are all read from the benchmark JSON file so you can refresh the age comparisons every year without rewriting the page.
3
Targets Matter Too
Some goals are about peers, and some are about planning milestones. This page checks both, using emergency fund months and savings multiples alongside age-band median and average values.
4
High-Interest Debt Gets Heavy Weight
Consumer debt, tight fixed obligations, and weak cash buffers drag the score harder than small differences in income because they directly affect resilience and decision-making power.
5
Protection Is Part of Wealth
Insurance, beneficiary updates, employer match capture, and basic estate planning do not always show up in net worth today, but they matter enormously for overall financial health.
6
Useful Feedback Beats Pretty Numbers
The goal is not just a grade. The page also surfaces the strengths worth protecting, the biggest leaks, and the next few actions that would improve the score fastest.
